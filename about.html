Villa Amparo History

Villa Amparo Beach has been a cherished part of coastal living since its inception in the summer of 1978. Founded by `Maria and Carlos Rodriguez', a visionary couple with a deep love for the sea and a dream of creating a seaside haven, the company started as a quaint, family-run beachside retreat. Maria and Carlos opened their doors to guests seeking solace and serenity by the shore. What began as a modest villa with a few cozy cottages soon expanded to include a vibrant community of vacation homes, each designed with a blend of comfort and rustic charm. Over the years, Villa Amparo Beach Company has become a cherished destination for those seeking refuge from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Carlos A. Rodriguez Sr. (Founder)

Carlos Antonio Rodriguez, the visionary behind the beloved Villa Amparo Beach Company, had a rich and diverse life before embarking on his journey to create a coastal paradise. Born into a coastal community, Carlos developed a deep appreciation for the sea and nature from a young age. He spent his early years working as a fisherman, forging a profound connection with the ocean that would later inspire his life's work. Carlos also explored various trades and crafts, learning valuable skills that would prove instrumental in the construction and design of his beach resort. His unwavering passion for preserving the natural beauty of the shoreline and his commitment to hospitality were the cornerstones of his success, setting the stage for the creation of Villa Amparo, a legacy that continues to flourish.

Maria R. Rodriguez (Founder)

Maria Elena Ramirez, the co-founder of the esteemed Villa Amparo Beach Company, had a remarkable life filled with diverse experiences before embarking on her journey to create the seaside haven that is now known and loved by many. Raised in a coastal town, Maria's childhood was imbued with the rhythms of the ocean and a deep appreciation for nature. She honed her talents as an artist and gardener, skills that would later contribute to the stunning aesthetics of Villa Amparo. Maria also pursued a career in hospitality, working in various hotels and resorts, where she developed a keen understanding of guest needs and a commitment to exceptional service. Her marriage to Carlos Rodriguez was a union of shared dreams and aspirations, ultimately leading to the establishment of Villa Amparo. Maria's creative flair, love for the environment, and dedication to making guests feel at home have left an indelible mark on the resort's character and charm, creating a legacy of warmth and beauty by the sea.
